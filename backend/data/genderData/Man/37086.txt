 (CNN) Here’s a perfect one for a Throwback Thursday: Adam West, Burt Ward and Julie Newmar are reprising their famous ”Batman” roles in the new animated film ”Batman: Return of the Caped Crusaders.” The trio starred in the 1960s television story with West starring as Batman, Ward as Robin, and Newmar as the villainous Catwoman. Entertainment Weekly premiered the trailer for the movie Tuesday.  West celebrated by retweeting Warner Bros. Home Entertainment’s tweet about the trailer and adding ”I’m back!” Warner Bros.
