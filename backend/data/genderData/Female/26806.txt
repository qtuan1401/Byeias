Thursday on CNN’s “The Lead,” Senate Minority Leader Senator Harry Reid ( ) told network correspondent Manu Raju a a brokered convention on the Democratic side would be “fun” and possible.  Partial transcript as follows: RAJU: Do you think there’s any possibility that they could be going into a Democratic convention without a consensus nominee? REID: I don’t know, but it would be kind of fun. RAJU: I mean is it possible for a brokered convention? REID: Sure, sure.
