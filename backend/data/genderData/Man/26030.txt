RIO GRANDE CITY, Texas  —   A    boy and his father are the latest victims of   drug violence in Texas after a team of gunmen shot at them multiple times before making a clean getaway. [The murder took place on Sunday evening when Starr County Sheriff’s Deputies responded to the intersection of Woods Brothers Road and Segunada Road, information provided to Breitbart Texas by authorities revealed.  At the scene, authorities found the body of    Hector “El Calzon or Bibi” Garcia Jr. nearby, authorities also found his    son Julian who had been struck by gunfire. Emergency medical personnel rushed the boy to a local hospital where he died shortly after.  According to preliminary information provided to Breitbart Texas by the Starr County Sheriff’s Office, the double homicide was carried out by a team of gunmen who had been riding in a four door, Ford   truck.
