Eight people were wounded —  one critically —  after a gunman or multiple gunmen opened fire during a Ja. 16 rally in Miami celebrating Rev. Martin Luther King, say multiple news reports. [The incident occurred around 3:40 p. m. near Martin Luther King Jr. Memorial Park.  NBC 6 reports the wounded range between 11 years in age and 30.
