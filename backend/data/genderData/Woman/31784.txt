 (CNN) On a night where modern musical ”La La Land” won big during the Golden Globes, Disney gave moviegoers a new sneak peek of the next movie that could sweep people off their feet: the upcoming   ”Beauty and the Beast.” In a trailer that debuted during the award show, viewers were given the first extended look at Emma Watson showing off her singing skills in the movie.  Watson can be heard singing ”Belle” in the   promo. Its debut came just a few hours after Watson shared a new poster for the film on her official Twitter account.  The movie opens March 17.
