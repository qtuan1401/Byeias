New Delhi (CNN) A bomber detonated explosives Friday near Dhaka’s airport in Bangladesh, killing himself but causing no other casualties, police officials told CNN.  The blast occurred at a checkpoint near Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport, according to Assistant Police Commissioner Ruhul Amin Shagor. It was the third recent attempted suicide attack in Bangladesh’s capital.  On Saturday, a suspected militant carrying explosives was killed by the Rapid Action Battalion, or RAB, Bangladesh’s counterterrorism squad.  On March 17, a suicide bomber denotated explosives outside a RAB camp in Dhaka, killing himself and injuring two security personnel.
