UCLA gunman Mainak Sarkar allegedly shot and killed professor William Klug on Wednesday over a computer code. Breitbart News Previously reported that Sarkar allegedly shot and killed Klug in a gun free zone just before 10 am on Wednesday, then turned the gun on himself —  taking his own life. Any motives beyond the allegations related to the computer code are unknown at this time, but   reports that police are “[focused] on Sarkar’s grades and a “ ” poor relationship between the two men. ” Sarkar was one of Klug’s past students.
