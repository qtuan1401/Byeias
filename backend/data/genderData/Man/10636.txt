The Times of Israel reports: BEIRUT, Lebanon  —   Dozens of Syrian students on Saturday gathered outside the offices of the United Nations in Damascus to protest a US missile attack on an air base. The protesters held banners and chanted “Death to America” and “Death to Israel. ”[One of the banners they carried read: “The Iraqi scenario will not be repeated in Syria. ” They were referring to the 2003   invasion of Iraq after Washington accused Saddam Hussein of hiding weapons of mass destruction  —   a belief that later turned out to be incorrect.  University student Ashraf Fadel said he came to denounce “the unjust American aggression against Syria. ” He added that the UN was “created to support America instead of serving the wronged people. ” Tensions skyrocketed this week after an alleged chemical weapons attack by the Syrian government killed dozens of people.
