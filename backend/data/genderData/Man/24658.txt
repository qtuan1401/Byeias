Sony Pictures has released the international trailer for the upcoming   Ghostbusters reboot. [The film stars Kristen Wiig and Melissa McCarthy as scientists Erin Gilbert and Abby Yates, according to ComingSoon. net. The pair then teams up with nuclear engineer Jillian Holtzmann (Kate McKinnon) and Patty Tolan (Leslie Jones) to take on the paranormal.  The film also features Chris Hemsworth, Andy Garcia, and Michael K. Williams. Additionally, original cast members Bill Murray, Dan Aykroyd, Ernie Hudson, Sigourney Weaver, and Annie Potts will have cameos in the reboot. Hudson, who played the role of Winston in the original films, initially criticized the   sequel in 2014. After taking heat online, the actor later described the cast as “phenomenal. ” Ghostbusters will debut on Jul. 15.
