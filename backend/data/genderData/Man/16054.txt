CHEROKEE, Iowa  —   Republican presidential candidate Sen. Ted Cruz ( ) called President Barack Obama’s administration “the most   administration in the history of our country. ”[Cruz, who is currently on a   bus tour through Iowa, spoke to reporters about Obama’s announcement of new gun control executive actions.  “This has been the most   administration in the history of our country” Cruz said. “This has been an administration that has targeted law abiding citizens in particular, rather than targeting criminals, rather than targeting terrorists. ” “After seven years we’ve seen a pattern of repeated abuse of executive power from this president, repeated refusal to follow federal law or follow the constitution” Cruz said.
