A Salafi terrorist group associated with Islamic State claimed responsibility for the volley of rockets fired at southern Israel on Friday night. [“We take responsibility for the rocket bombardment on the Sderot settlement at 11:07 p. m. Friday night. Praised God, we allowed for Jewish blood [to flow] and we turned their night to day. We activated alarms in all of southern occupied Palestine,” read a statement allegedly issued by group Ajnad Bayt  .  Five rockets were fired from the Gaza Strip at Israel on Friday night, with two of the projectiles landing in Israeli territory.
