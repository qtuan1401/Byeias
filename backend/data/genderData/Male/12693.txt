The national unemployment rate in December ticked upward to 4. 7 percent, although the United States economy added 156, 000 jobs, according to statistics released by the Labor Department.[  The labor force participation rate rose to 62. 7 percent in December, up from 62. 6 in November. A total of 95, 102, 000 Americans are not in the work force, up only 18 since November.
