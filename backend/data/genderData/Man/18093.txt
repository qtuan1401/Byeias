Karen Worcester, executive director of Wreaths Across America, told Breitbart News Daily SiriusXM host Alex Marlow on Friday, “There are more than 1. 2 million wreaths en route to locations where veterans are buried all over the country, with incredible teams of drivers getting these wreaths out to be placed by more than a million volunteers. ”[December 17 is National Wreaths Across America Day. “I just really appreciate your giving us a platform to reach out and tell people about what we do,” said Worcester.  Wreaths Across America is also an education program, “teaching about the lives and sacrifices of those that have given us this freedom,” Worcester said. “There will be 70, 000 people” at Arlington laying wreaths tomorrow, she added. While the group has met its goals for this year, it is not too early to begin contributing wreaths or donations for next year via the group’s website.
