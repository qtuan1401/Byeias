On this weekend’s broadcast of Fox News Channel’s “Sunday Morning Futures,” Rep. Jason Chaffetz ( ) chairman of the House Oversight Committee, told host Maria Bartiromo he intends to continue the investigation into Hillary Clinton and her use of a private server while secretary of state.  “I’d be derelict in my duty if I just let it go and said, ‘Well, there’s an election,’” Chaffetz said.
