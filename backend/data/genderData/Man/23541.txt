President Barack Obama kicked off his vacation at Martha’s Vineyard this afternoon with another round of golf. [According to CBS reporter Mark Knoller, that marks Obama’s 300th golf game during his presidency.  Obama reached this presidential milestone at the Farm Neck golf course with NBA star Chris Paul, his friend Cy Walker,  and White House staffer Joe Paulsen. President George W. Bush played golf 24 times during eight years in the White House, intentionally suspending the recreational activity in August 2003. President Woodrow Wilson reportedly played 1, 200 rounds of golf as president, while President Dwight Eisenhower played 800.
