Shocking surveillance footage taken outside a store in Venice Beach, CA on Sunday shows the moment a man brutally punched a woman in the face, reports Daily Mail.  The video begins with a man and woman leaving the store arguing. The two walk off with the man in front. He then turns around and punches the woman in the face. She fell down to the ground while people in the crowd try to restrain the man, but he was able to escape. According to Live Leak, the two did not know each other, but had seen each other earlier in the day. Los Angeles police are still looking for the man in the video.
