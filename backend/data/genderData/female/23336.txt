According to WILX, authorities in Tennessee are investigating a woman for posting a photo of her two young children with duct tape over their hands and mouths on Facebook.  Facebook user Jaton Justsilly Jaibabi uploaded the photo, along with the caption: “Kids for sale, 45% off because they bad. ” The photo of the young children went viral, prompting Memphis Police and Tennessee Department of Children’s Services to investigate the incident. The woman’s cousin claims it was all a joke and people just overreacted. He also added that the kids are safe and loved, but the mother has learned her lesson.
