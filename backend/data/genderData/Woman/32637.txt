March 6, 2010   Visits British troops in Afghanistan and promises them 200 new patrol vehicles more resistant to roadside bombs. May 11, 2010   Resigns as prime minister after the Labour Party comes in second in parliamentary elections held May 6. He will stay on as the MP for Kirkcaldy and Cowdenbeath.
