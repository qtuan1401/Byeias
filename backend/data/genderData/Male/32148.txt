 (CNN) Stephen Colbert can add another gig to his resume. The host of ”The Late Show with Stephen Colbert,” will helm the 69th Primetime Emmy Awards on September 17.  ”Stephen is the ultimate master of ceremonies with   creative talents, and as we’ve seen the past few months, he has a fearless passion for live television,” read an announcement from CBS    the network home of Colbert’s nightly show, as well as the next Primetime Emmy broadcast.
