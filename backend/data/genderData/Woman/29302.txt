Breitbart News Senior    author of Clinton Cash and Government Accountability Institute President Peter Schweizer spoke with The Wall Street Journal about the WikiLeaks email dump that show an arranged $12 million donation from Moroccan King Mohammed VI to the Clinton Foundation in 2014 in return for the Clinton Global Initiative hosting its international meeting in Morocco.  Schweizer called the agreement further proof of “quid pro quo” by the Clinton Foundation. He said, “The reason I think this is so troubling is, first of all, it does indicate pretty clearly there was a ‘quid pro quo’ here.
