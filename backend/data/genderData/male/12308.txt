Starlord, Gamora, Drax, Rocket, and Groot face off against villain Thanos in Marvel’s Guardians of the Galaxy: The Telltale Series. [Telltale Games, the developer behind the critically acclaimed episodic adventure video game adaptations of The Walking Dead, The Wolf Among Us, and Game of Thrones, is tackling Marvel’s Guardians of the Galaxy just in time for the theatrical release of Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2. Each of the Guardians has a reason to desire this relic, as does a ruthless enemy who will stop at nothing to tear it from their hands.
