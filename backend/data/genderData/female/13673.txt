The California Institute of Technology is reporting approximately 100 earthquakes were detected near the   border on New Year’s Eve. [The quakes were centered about Brawley, CA, “which is about 125 miles east of San Diego and 20 miles north of the border. ”  According to ABC 7, the largest of the quakes was about 3. 9 magnitude. They report that scientists say larger quakes in the region are possible and “the shaking may continue for several days. ” The outbreak of quakes occurred over 30 miles from the San Andreas Fault and is not expected to cause any large scale quakes.
