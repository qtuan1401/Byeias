Facebook has released a   collection of new animated   stickers. [“Squad Goals” says one sticker along with a picture of the four main cast members. “YASSS” says another featuring one of the new Ghostbusters pointing a glowing weapon in the air.  Other stickers in the collection include the Marshmallow man shouting “On Fleek,” Slimer holding his belly whilst saying “HAHA” and another one of the new Ghostbusters holding her hand over her mouth with the caption “OMG” above. The pack is available for free in Facebook’s sticker store, and the new animated pictures can be used all over the social network including in messages, comments, and statuses.
