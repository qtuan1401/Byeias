Sen. Ted Cruz ( ) will be skipping President Obama’s State of the Union address Tuesday. [Cruz will be in New Hampshire campaigning during Obama’s speech. He’s going to be in New Hampshire, working hard to win New Hampshire,’ Tyler told The Dallas Morning News. ‘No disrespect. It just is going to work out this way.’  Cruz’s campaign rivals, Sen.
