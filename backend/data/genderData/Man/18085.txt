Six people were run down and injured by an escaped bull in Texas after the beast broke free from a veterinary clinic. The incident occurred south of Fort Worth in the Alvarado. The bull was put down by authorities.   The matter was first reported by Fox4News. com and they wrote: It hurt a total of six people. One man ended up in crutches and another was at the wrong end of the horns. “He started coming towards me. And I turned. And as I turned he hit me right here  …     took me down. And that’s all I remember,” Frank Ludovico, Jr. said.
