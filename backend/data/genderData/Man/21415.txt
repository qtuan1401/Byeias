At a campaign appearance for Democratic presidential hopeful Hillary Clinton on Saturday in Concord, NH, former Secretary of State Madeleine Albright at some harsh words for women not voting for Clinton in the upcoming primary.  Albright said there was a “special place in hell” for women not helping other women out, which was caught on camera by Fox News and aired on Saturday. “We can tell our story about how we climbed the ladder and a lot of you younger women don’t think you have to  —   it’s been done,” Albright said. “It’s not done, and you have to help. Hillary Clinton will always be there for you. And just remember, there’s a special place in hell for women who don’t help each other. ” (  American Mirror) Follow Jeff Poor on Twitter @jeff_poor
