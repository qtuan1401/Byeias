At Conservative Review, David Bozell takes House Speaker Paul Ryan to task over President Obama’s Iran deal.  David Bozell, the president of For America, lit into the speaker’s lackluster response. Bozell told Conservative Review, in exclusive remarks, “It is Congressional malpractice the Obama administration was able to send $400 million in taxpayer dollars to the world’s top rated state sponsor of terrorism. ” “Where was the oversight from the Speaker of the House?” Bozell continued. “As third in line for the Presidency, Speaker Ryan should have been aware of these hostage negotiations. The fact that the Speaker’s office did nothing tells me one of two things: Either Paul Ryan didn’t know, which makes him incompetent or he did know, which makes him complicit. ” Read the whole thing.
